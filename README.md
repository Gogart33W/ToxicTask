# 💀 ToxicTask — The Ultimate Productivity Bully

**ToxicTask** is not your typical "stay organized" app. It's a task manager that **insults you into being a GIGACHAD.** If you like to procrastinate, this app will make your life a living hell until you get your work done.

## 🚀 Why ToxicTask?
Most apps are too nice. "Please, finish your task." **Forget that.** 
ToxicTask uses:
- **Zero-Mercy Reminders:** Precise `AlarmClock` triggers that wake up your phone to grill you.
- **Toxicity Levels:** From "Mild Slacker" to "Extreme Personal Insults."
- **Gigachad Ranking:** Earn respect by finishing tasks, or stay a "Lox" forever.
- **Deep System Integration:** Works in the background like a digital stalker.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Language:** 100% Kotlin (Modern & Fast)
- **UI:** Jetpack Compose (Declarative & Sexy)
- **Database:** Room (Offline-first)
- **Engine:** Custom `AlarmManager` & `BroadcastReceiver` system for "clock-like" precision.
- **Persistence:** Jetpack DataStore.

## 📦 Installation
1. Clone this repo.
2. Build in Android Studio (Icarus/Koala+ recommended).
3. Enable "POST_NOTIFICATIONS" and "Alarm" permissions.
4. Prepare for the roast.
