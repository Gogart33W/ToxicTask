Privacy Policy for ToxicTask
Last updated: May 2026
Overview
ToxicTask ("the App") is a productivity and task management application developed by Gogart33W. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.
Short version: ToxicTask does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection
ToxicTask does not collect any personal information. The App does not:

Connect to any server or the internet
Send any data to the developer or third parties
Collect analytics or crash reports
Use advertising SDKs
Require account registration


Data Stored Locally on Your Device
All data created within the App is stored exclusively on your device using:

Room Database — stores your tasks, priorities, and completion status
DataStore Preferences — stores your app settings (theme, language, notification preferences, toxicity level)

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by the App.

Notifications
The App uses Android's notification system to send you productivity reminders at intervals you configure. These notifications are generated entirely on-device based on your task data. No data is transmitted externally to generate these notifications.

Permissions Used
PermissionPurposePOST_NOTIFICATIONSTo display productivity reminder notificationsSCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARMTo schedule precise notification timesRECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETEDTo reschedule alarms after device restartWAKE_LOCKTo ensure alarms fire on time
None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

Children's Privacy
ToxicTask is not directed at children under 13. The App contains motivational content with strong language ("Toxicity" feature). Users should be 13 or older.

Changes to This Policy
If the App is updated to include features that affect data handling, this policy will be updated accordingly. The latest version is always available at this URL.
